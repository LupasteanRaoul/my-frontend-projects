About Global Exhibit

Global Exhibit is a curated photography platform showcasing the world's most beautiful locations through the lenses of talented photographers from around the globe. Our mission is to inspire travel, appreciation for nature, and cultural understanding through stunning visual storytelling.

Founded in 2015, we've grown to become one of the largest online photography communities, with contributors from over 140 countries and millions of visitors each month.
